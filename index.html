Hi! I'm Suphakan Sukwong. I usually go with Suphakan, but not a lot of people know that my nickname is Melon(that's what my parents call me). I'm from Bangkok, Thailand, but currently in Colorado, USA pursuing Bachelor's degree. I was lucky enough to find out what I want to do since I was around 8 years old. I saw an airplane in the sky and wondered what kept it from falling to the ground - because everything else falls! That curiosity made me want to become a pilot and was the beginning of my passion in aviation. I would say I wasn't sure if I was afraid of height or something else because I wasn't feel comfortable when I stood at the edge of the cliff. That would kill my dream entirely. Fortunately, now that I have learned to paraglide and skydive, I would say I was afraid of death, not height, because now I'm comfortable doing those activities but still not comfortable standing at the edge of the cliff. My next goal is to fly an airplane, my childhood dream. Up to this point, it will be no surprise if I tell you that I'm a junior in Aerospace Engineering. Because I'm an engineering major, I had to take basic programming class. And that was when I discovered my another interest, coding. Arguably, I think I love coding because it takes similar logical thinking as engineering. That is why I'm also a Computer Science minor in addition to Aerospace major.
